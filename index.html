About Coney Cones

Coney Cones founded in 1938 in St.Paul Minnesota has brought many generations joy. We started in St.Paul but now has eleven stores in the Twin Cities area. Run now by the fourth generation Johnson family, Coney Cones has thrived over the years supplying fresh ice cream from local dairy farms. We strive to bring you the freshest and most delicious ice cream everytime and for the past 75 plus years we have done just that.
